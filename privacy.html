Minda Mental Health — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Minda Mental Health ("we", "us", or "our") is a mental health support application operated by Cem G. ("the developer"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address (used for account creation and authentication)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Preferred language

2.2 User Content

  • Chat messages you send to Minda
  • Mood entries (mood score, optional notes)
  • Thought records (situation, thought, emotion, reframe)
  • Usage data (messages sent, features used)

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Language preference
  • Timestamps of interactions

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 AI-Powered Conversations

Your chat messages are sent to a third-party AI service (OpenRouter, using the GLM-5.2 model) to generate supportive responses. The AI service processes your messages to provide mental health support conversations.

3.2 Service Provision

  • To provide mental health support, mood tracking, and thought records
  • To maintain conversation history
  • To send crisis resources when needed
  • To improve our service quality

3.3 Analytics

We collect aggregated, anonymized usage statistics to understand app usage patterns and improve our service.

4. Third-Party AI Service Disclosure

Minda uses OpenRouter (https://openrouter.ai) as its AI service provider. When you send a message in the chat:

  • Your message is transmitted to OpenRouter's servers
  • OpenRouter processes the message using the GLM-5.2 language model
  • The AI-generated response is returned to you
  • Both your message and the AI response are stored in our database

OpenRouter's privacy policy is available at: https://openrouter.ai/privacy

We do not control OpenRouter's data retention policies. Please review their privacy policy to understand how they handle data.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored on our secure servers located in the European Union.

5.2 Security Measures

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • Data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • JWT tokens for authentication
  • Database access is restricted and authenticated
  • Regular security updates

5.3 Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile screen.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of Access: You can request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You can delete your account and all associated data at any time within the app, or request deletion by contacting us.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your personal data.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent for data processing at any time by deleting your account.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We only share data:

  • With OpenRouter (AI service provider) for generating chat responses
  • When required by law or legal proceedings
  • In aggregated, anonymized form for analytics

8. Children's Privacy

Minda is not intended for children under 17. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 17. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 17, please contact us.

9. International Users

If you access Minda from outside the European Union, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the EU. By using Minda, you consent to this transfer.

10. Crisis and Emergency Situations

In crisis situations, Minda provides emergency hotline numbers based on your language preference. Minda does not store or transmit crisis-related data beyond normal chat history. If you are in immediate danger, please contact your local emergency services directly.

11. Medical Disclaimer

Minda is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of a qualified healthcare provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition. Never disregard professional medical advice or delay in seeking it because of something you have read in this app.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
